About ASOT

The Apostolic School of Theology exists to preserve Apostolic doctrine and equip believers for effective ministry, discipleship, and evangelism.

Our Mission

To provide doctrinally sound, Bible-based Apostolic training using a modern online platform.

Our Distinctives
