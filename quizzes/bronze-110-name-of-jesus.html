Quiz Questions

1. In your own words, explain why the Name of Jesus is central to Apostolic doctrine and practice.

2. True or False: According to Acts 4:12, there are many names under heaven given among men whereby we must be saved.

3. Fill in the blank: “Neither is there salvation in any other: for there is none other ______ under heaven given among men, whereby we must be saved.”

4. Which passage declares that “at the name of Jesus every knee should bow”?

5. Briefly explain how the Name of Jesus relates to the revelation of the oneness of God.

6. True or False: Apostolic believers baptize “in the Name of Jesus” because the Name of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost is revealed in that one Name.

7. Fill in the blank: Colossians 3:17 says, “And whatsoever ye do in word or deed, do all in the ______ of the Lord Jesus.”

8. Which statement best describes the authority of the Name of Jesus?

9. Give one example from Acts where miracles or deliverance occurred through the Name of Jesus.

10. True or False: Using the Name of Jesus without relationship, faith, or obedience can be dangerous (for example, the sons of Sceva in Acts 19).

11. Briefly explain why prayer is commanded to be offered in the Name of Jesus.

12. Which of the following best explains why Apostolic believers emphasize the Name of Jesus in water baptism?

13. Fill in the blank: The Name “Jesus” means “Yahweh is ______” or “The LORD is ______.”

14. True or False: The Name of Jesus is only important for salvation, but has no relevance for daily Christian living.

15. Briefly describe how the Name of Jesus should influence a believer’s conduct, speech, and ministry.

16. Which passage in Acts shows that baptism “in the name of the Lord Jesus” was practiced among Gentile believers?

17. Briefly explain why demons and spiritual forces recognize and fear the authority of the Name of Jesus.

18. True or False: The exaltation of Jesus’ Name is connected to His obedience unto death and His resurrection.

19. Give one practical example of how an Apostolic minister or believer can honor the Name of Jesus in public ministry.

20. In two or three sentences, summarize the Apostolic understanding of the Name of Jesus and why it is the final and fullest revelation of God for salvation.
