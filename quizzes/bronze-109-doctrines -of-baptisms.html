Quiz Questions

1. List the three main “baptisms” emphasized in Apostolic doctrine (water, Spirit, and fire).

2. True or False: The New Testament allows any baptismal formula as long as the person is sincere.

3. Fill in the blank: According to Acts 2:38, believers are to be baptized “in the name of ______ ______ for the remission of sins.”

4. Which passage most clearly shows the necessity of water and Spirit for entering the Kingdom of God?

5. Briefly explain the significance of water baptism in Jesus’ name in relation to Christ’s death, burial, and resurrection.

6. True or False: The baptism of the Holy Ghost is accompanied in Acts by the initial evidence of speaking with other tongues.

7. Fill in the blank: John the Baptist said, “He shall baptize you with the Holy Ghost, and with ______.”

8. Which statement best describes the “baptism of fire”?

9. Explain how the “doctrines of baptisms” relate to the believer’s identification with Christ and ongoing sanctification.

10. True or False: The Book of Acts consistently shows baptism being administered in the name of Jesus Christ or the Lord Jesus.

11. Give one example (book and reference) where believers received the Holy Ghost with the evidence of tongues.

12. Which of the following best describes proper water baptism?

13. Briefly explain why re-baptism may be necessary in some cases (for example, if prior baptism was not in Jesus’ name or before true repentance).

14. True or False: The doctrine of baptisms is part of the foundation listed in Hebrews 6:1–2.

15. Fill in the blank: The Holy Ghost baptism empowers believers to be ______ unto Christ, enabling boldness and effectiveness in ministry.

16. Describe how baptism “into Christ” changes a believer’s identity and relationship to sin.

17. Which of the following is a practical response to the doctrine of baptisms?

18. True or False: Baptism, without faith and repentance, automatically saves a person.

19. Briefly explain how trials and fiery tests (baptism of fire) contribute to spiritual maturity.

20. In two or three sentences, summarize the Apostolic understanding of the doctrines of baptisms and why they are central to the New Testament church.
