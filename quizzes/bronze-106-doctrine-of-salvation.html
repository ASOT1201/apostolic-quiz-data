Quiz Questions

1. In your own words, define “salvation” from a biblical (Apostolic) perspective.

2. True or False: According to Apostolic doctrine, salvation is by grace through faith, but that grace produces obedience and a changed life.

3. Fill in the blank: “For by ______ are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God.” (Ephesians 2:8)

4. Which passage is most commonly used to summarize the New Testament plan of salvation preached on the Day of Pentecost?

5. Briefly list and describe the three main steps of the Apostolic New Birth experience (hint: repentance, water baptism, Spirit baptism).

6. True or False: In Apostolic teaching, repentance is only feeling sorry and does not require a change of mind or direction.

7. Fill in the blank: Water baptism in the name of ______ ______ is for the remission of sins and identifies the believer with Christ’s death, burial, and resurrection.

8. Which of the following is the Apostolic formula for baptism?

9. Describe why the baptism of the Holy Ghost, with the initial evidence of speaking in tongues, is considered essential in Apostolic soteriology.

10. True or False: In Apostolic doctrine, “believing” includes obedience to the gospel, not mental belief alone.

11. Fill in the blank: The gospel is the good news of Christ’s ______, ______, and ______ (1 Corinthians 15:1–4).

12. Which statement best describes the relationship between grace and works in Apostolic teaching?

13. Briefly explain why genuine repentance is necessary before water baptism and Spirit baptism.

14. True or False: The doctrine of salvation includes both what Christ has done for us and what the Holy Ghost does in us.

15. List at least two scriptures (book and reference) that show the connection between obedience and salvation.

16. Which of the following best describes “justification”?

17. Briefly define “sanctification” and explain its relationship to salvation.

18. True or False: Apostolic believers teach that once a person is saved, there is no need to continue in faith, holiness, or obedience.

19. Explain how the fear of the Lord and love for God both motivate a believer to live out their salvation.

20. In two or three sentences, summarize the Apostolic doctrine of salvation and why it is vital for preaching, teaching, and evangelism.
