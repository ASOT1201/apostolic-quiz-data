Quiz Questions

1. Quote or summarize 1 Timothy 3:16 as it relates to “the mystery of godliness.”

2. True or False: In Apostolic Oneness theology, “mystery” means something that can never be known or revealed.

3. According to Apostolic teaching, how is God “manifest in the flesh” in the person of Jesus Christ?

4. Which passage emphasizes that in Christ dwells all the fullness of the Godhead bodily?

5. Explain the difference between the “mystery of godliness” and the “mystery of iniquity.”

6. Fill in the blank: “God was manifest in the ________, justified in the Spirit, seen of angels, preached unto the Gentiles…”

7. True or False: The mystery of godliness reveals that Jesus is a lesser deity under a higher God.

8. How does the doctrine of the Incarnation (God in Christ) protect the church from both idolatry and unbelief?

9. Which title best expresses the fullness of Jesus’ identity in Oneness theology?

10. In your own words, describe how the Old Testament “Hear, O Israel, the LORD our God is one LORD” (Deut. 6:4) connects with the mystery of godliness.

11. Fill in the blank: “Great is the ________ of godliness: God was manifest in the flesh…”

12. Explain how Jesus can be both fully God and fully man without being two separate persons.

13. True or False: The “mystery of godliness” includes the revelation that godliness is impossible without Christ dwelling in us.

14. Which statement best reflects Oneness understanding of the Father and the Son?

15. How does the revelation of who Jesus is affect the way we pray, worship, and baptize?

16. Fill in the blank: The mystery of godliness teaches that God was “believed on in the world” and “received up into ________.”

17. True or False: According to Apostolic theology, understanding the mystery of godliness is optional and not necessary for sound doctrine.

18. Which New Testament passage emphasizes that God was in Christ reconciling the world unto Himself?

19. In what ways does the mystery of godliness inspire holy living and reverence in the life of the believer?

20. Briefly summarize, in two or three sentences, what “the mystery of godliness” means to you as an Apostolic believer.
