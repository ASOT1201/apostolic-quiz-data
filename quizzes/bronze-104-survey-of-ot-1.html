Quiz Questions

1. Briefly define what is meant by a “survey of the Old Testament.”

2. True or False: The Old Testament is primarily written in Hebrew, with some sections in Aramaic.

3. List the four main divisions of the Protestant Old Testament (for example: Law, ______, ______, ______).

4. Which books are commonly known as the “Pentateuch” or “Torah”?

5. Fill in the blank: The book of ________ records the creation, early human history, and the beginnings of God’s covenant with Abraham.

6. True or False: The historical books (Joshua through Esther) describe Israel’s entrance into, life in, and loss of the Promised Land.

7. Briefly describe the main theme of the book of Exodus.

8. Which of the following best describes the purpose of Leviticus?

9. In a sentence or two, explain why Deuteronomy is important for understanding Israel’s covenant relationship with God.

10. True or False: The books of Joshua, Judges, Ruth, 1–2 Samuel, and 1–2 Kings are part of the historical section of the Old Testament.

11. Describe the overall spiritual pattern seen in the book of Judges (for example, sin, oppression, crying out, deliverance).

12. Which of the following best describes the books of 1 & 2 Samuel?

13. Name at least two key figures from the early historical books (Joshua through 2 Kings) and briefly state their significance.

14. True or False: The books of Ezra and Nehemiah focus on the return from Babylonian exile and the rebuilding of the temple and walls of Jerusalem.

15. Briefly explain how the Old Testament points forward to Christ, even in the Law and historical books.

16. Fill in the blank: In a survey of the Old Testament, it is important to pay attention to key ________, key ________, and key ________.

17. Which of the following is a good reason for Apostolic ministers to study an Old Testament survey?

18. Give one example of how misunderstanding the Old Testament background can lead to misinterpreting a New Testament passage.

19. True or False: The Old Testament has no relevance for New Testament believers today.

20. In two or three sentences, explain why a solid survey of the Old Testament is essential for Apostolic preaching and teaching.
